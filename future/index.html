ABOUT
The Bridge Papers is a groundbreaking experiment in citizen journalism. In an era when the power of the media grows in lockstep with the polarization of politics, the American people consistently seem to be divided between ourselves, due in part to the news sources we choose to consume and the issues we choose to care about. For most of us, the truth is that there are entirely hidden subcultures and issues around us, ones that we have never thought to explore.
