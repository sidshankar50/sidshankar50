ABOUT
The Bridge Papers is an experiment in citizen journalism. In an era when the power of the media grows in lockstep with the polarization of politics, the American people consistently seem to be divided between ourselves, due in part to the news sources we choose to consume and the issues we choose to care about. For most of us, the truth is that there are entirely hidden subcultures and issues around us, ones that we have never thought to explore. Our mission is to bring you those stories.

THE BRONZE SOLDIER
The Cold War-era proxy conflicts that pitted NATO against the USSR in the Afghanistan, Nicaragua, and other places haven’t petered out. Instead, new digital versions are being waged every day in a format that would be genius if it wasn’t so malicious.
THE $500 BIONIC ARM
The future of prostheses is mind control. Cutting-edge labs are charging exorbitant prices for functionality that a 15-year-old can replicate in his basement workshop for a few hundred dollars. We interviewed this Nokia-Bell Labs science fair winner on his innovative proof-of-concept.
ALL FOR ONE
With topics ranging from flag design to the broken systems of American soccer, this first volume brings together eight parts of our global community. "All for one," the titular reference to Alexandre Dumas, draws every piece towards the theme of solidarity and self-improvement.
